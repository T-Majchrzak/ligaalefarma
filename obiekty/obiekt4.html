The Sugar Mill is a production building unlocked at experience level 7. A second Sugar Mill was added on 28 June 2021 and is unlocked at level 76. The first costs 350 💰 and takes 5 h 59 min (or 25 💎) to build. The second costs 200,000 💰 and takes 6 h (or 25 💎). Both give players 13 ⭐ when completed.
